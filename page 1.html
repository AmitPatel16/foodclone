These Creamy Dill Cucumber Toasties are a simple and delicious summertime snack! They only require a few ingredients and can be made in just a few minutes. The cream cheese spread is flavored with dill and garlic and pairs perfectly with the thinly-sliced cucumbers. Although they would also be delicious as a light lunch or mid-afternoon snack, I prefer to serve these Toasties as an appetizer or side dish. If you’re looking for a refreshing and flavorful way to enjoy cucumbers, give these Creamy Dill Cucumber Toasties a try!

Thai Style Shrimp is a delicious and easy-to-make recipe that is perfect for any occasion. The shrimp is marinated in a combination of fish sauce, garlic, ginger, and lime juice, which gives it a unique and flavorful taste. The shrimp is then cooked in a wok or frying pan until it is slightly browned and just cooked through. This dish can serve with rice or noodles and will please everyone at the table.

Sliders with ham and cheese are the ideal party dish since they’re delicious and simple to make. But what should you serve with them? Grilled asparagus, pasta salad, or roasted potatoes are all great options. Grilled asparagus is a healthy option that pairs well with the richness of the ham and cheese. Pasta salad is always a hit, and it’s easy to make ahead of time. Roasted potatoes are a hearty side that will fill everyone up.
